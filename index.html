The Camera Controlled Wheelchair -

Presented by some random high school students

The Camera Controlled Wheelchair (CAM-CTRL-WC) is designed for people who are unable to use their hands very well. All the user needs to look at the screen and set the cusor location and then press a button.

How is it made?

Our project uses python to track where the user is looking and move the bot in the spesfic direction.

  • Raspberry Pi 3B+
